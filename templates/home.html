SmartCoke

Coal to Coke - Prediction of the optimized blend and quality using Artificial Intelligence.

SMARTCOKE is suitable for Recovery Top charge , Recovery Stamp charge and Non Recovery Metcoke manufacturing processes


Developed in collaboration with Industantra

Abhitech Software Division

We develop cutting edge AI-based software to enhance efficiency and reduce cost, helping solve real-world industrial process challenges.


SMARTCOKE, our AI-driven metallurgical coke blend and quality optimisation software, helps coke manufacturers balance coal blends and process parameters to maintain quality while reducing costs.

SMARTCOKE uses advanced machine learning models and real-time data analysis. By incorporating logistical constraints like coal cost and availability, it determines the most efficient coal mix and operational parameters to produce high-quality coke while reducing cost and carbon footprint.
